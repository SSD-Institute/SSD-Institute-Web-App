Collaborative Voting

Collaborative voting is a sophisticated mechanism designed to overcome the limitations of traditional choose one electoral systems. By enabling participants to express a full spectrum of preferences across multiple options, it ensures that outcomes are not just majoritarian, but truly reflective of collective will and nuance. This approach significantly enhances the legitimacy of decisions, strengthens local governance, and promotes a more inclusive form of self-rule within any organizational or community structure.
